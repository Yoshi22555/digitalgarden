- Rhinophores are a type of an insect that is used as a farm animals that feeds off of the sunlight because their backs are made out of scales that are covered in chlorophyll
- Thanks to their specialised proboscis they can safely consume moisture from cacti.
- They are native species to the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert.
