- One of the main cities of [[Items & Concepts/terrestrial humanoids\|terrestrial humanoids]]
- Stronghold built by the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan Guild\|Cairan Guild]] which was created by the [[Characters/Cairan Family\|Cairan Family]], friends of [[Characters/Tenazir\|Tenazir]] who before he came were enslaved miners. They helped [[Characters/Tenazir\|Tenazir]] navigate the caves of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Asal Mountain\|Asal Mountain]].
- Located inside the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Sur\|Sur]] mountains
- Controls the mining operations of [[Items & Concepts/Salur\|Salur]]
- Most citizens are Dwarves
- People of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] have expanded into [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Plains and Forests/Gurun\|Gurun]] plains and created [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Silden\|Silden]], which later led to the creation of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Subur folder/Subur\|Subur]].
- The main religion is the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Surtur church\|Surtur church]] a religion that was formed after the [[Characters/Cairan Family\|Cairan Family]] saw [[Cosmology of Dilaria/Gods/Surtur\|Surtur]] create the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc. Landmarks/Artificial Sun\|Artificial Sun]]
- The stronghold is ruled by the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan Guild\|Cairan Guild]] which is controlled by the oldest members of the [[Characters/Cairan Family\|Cairan Family]]
 
Characteristic of the people of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]]:
- Most citizens are devoted to the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Surtur church\|Surtur church]].
- Because some of the oldest members of the [[Characters/Cairan Family\|Cairan Family]] still remember the harsh reality of being an enslaved miner, people of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] value personal freedom over anything else.
- People of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] are hard working and prefer physical labour over mental, however they are free and even encouraged to pursue any career path they want.
- They value familial connections a lot.
- They are the most xenophobic towards the [[Items & Concepts/subterranean races\|subterranean races]].
- Unless they are dealing with [[Items & Concepts/subterranean races\|subterranean races]] they tend to be more quiet and stoic.
