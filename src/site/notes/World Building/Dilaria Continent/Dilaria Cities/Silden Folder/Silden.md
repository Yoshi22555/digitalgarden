- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Silden\|Silden]] is located on the western coast of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Plains and Forests/Gurun\|Gurun]] plains.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Silden\|Silden]] was created by citizens of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]], mainly adventurers and wizards who believed that [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] didn't provide enough opportunities for growth.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Silden\|Silden]] is a mainly academical city, and its main export is knowledge, information and magical services.
- The city is divided into 8 districts, each based on a school of magic:
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Abjuration\|District of Abjuration]] - People from this district often end up as judges, lawyers (especially defence attorneys) or bureaucrats they are strict, methodical and precise.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Conjuration\|District of Conjuration]] - People from this district often end up as craftsmen, builders or artisans, they are hard working, stoic and patient. When the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Artifice\|District of Artifice]] was proposed, it was mostly people from the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Conjuration\|District of Conjuration]] who approved of it, which is why when [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Subur folder/Subur\|Subur]] was created, the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Conjuration\|District of Conjuration]] was the most affected by the emigration of artificers.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Divination\|District of Divination]] - People from this district often end up as bankers, information brokers or politicians, they are extremally attentive, perceptive and eloquent.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Enchantment\|District of Enchantment]] - People from this district often end up as interrogators, psychiatrists or scientists, they are very pragmatic, charitable and manipulative. They care more about the end goal and less about the means, they aren't beneath using enchantment magic to manipulate the minds of others to help them, even if the patient is opposed to that idea.  
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Evocation\|District of Evocation]] - People from this district often end up as elemental scientists, battlemages or performers, they are charismatic, impulsive and very direct.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Illusion\|District of Illusion]] - People from this district often end up as Spies, assassins and investigators, they are secretive, silent but trustworthy, it's often said that people from the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Illusion\|District of Illusion]] can't break a promise or a deal, even if they wanted to.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of  Necromancy\|District of  Necromancy]] - People from this district often end up as surgeons, gravediggers or gardeners, they are desensitized to death but also kind and understanding, they value nature a lot.
	- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Districts of Silden/District of Transmutation\|District of Transmutation]] - People from this district often end up as garbage collectors, recyclers or smiths, they are open to new experiences, penny-pinching and their relationships rarely last long (regardless of the type of the relationship)
- The city is governed by a collective of 8 representatives, 1 from each district, this collective is called [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/District Court\|District Court]]. Each representative is chosen by the people of their district, each district has its own way of choosing the representative which often is kept secret from the outsiders.
- Similarly to [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] most citizens worship [[Cosmology of Dilaria/Gods/Surtur\|Surtur]].
- Unlike in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia\|Lenturia]] in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Silden Folder/Silden\|Silden]] every race is allowed to study, regardless of their innate magical abilities.
