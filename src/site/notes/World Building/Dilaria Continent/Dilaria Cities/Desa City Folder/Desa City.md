- City created by merchants who wanted an easier access to the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc. Landmarks/Pele\|Pele]] island, which thanks to its resource density became an important destination for many merchants and adventurers.
- Despite originating from [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]], it has its own government consisting of the most important merchants of the city.
- Its relations with [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]] are usually friendly however, whenever merchants of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]] try to impose tariffs and duties on [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]] their relationship weakens and sometimes even becomes hostile to the point of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]] declaring war on [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]], this war didn't last long before signing a treaty but it has spoiled relationship between the citizens of these cities.

Characteristic of the people of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]]:
- There is no one central religion
- Most citizens are merchants or work for merchants
- Humans are the most common in Desa however there is more diversity than in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]]
- People of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]] are known to be greedy and manipulative, they are motivated by money. You shouldn't expect to receive anything for free in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]], not even a handful of sand.
- Despite their greed, they are very charitable and helpful, as long as being charitable doesn't cost them money.
- Many citizens of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Desa City Folder/Desa City\|Desa City]] dream of adventure especially one that involves the sea and lots of money.
