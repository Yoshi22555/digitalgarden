- Powerful sorcerers who entered a trance to focus their entire beings on upholding the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/thermal shield\|thermal shield]]. Unless some illness or something unexpected happens to [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenari\|Lenari]] they can stay in trance for decades or even centuries.
- They are the most respected group of people in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia\|Lenturia]], even Lenturians from [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia Slums\|Lenturia Slums]] respect them.
- They are guarded in secret hall inside the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenari Palace\|Lenari Palace]]. There have been few attempts at uncovering their location and disrupting their trance to shut down the defences of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia\|Lenturia]] but none succeeded.
