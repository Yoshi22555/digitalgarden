- The outer circular district of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia\|Lenturia]], where people without innate magical abilities live.
- Because it is the furthest district from [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Heart of Lenturia\|Heart of Lenturia]] the temperature here is the most prone to seasonal changes but it is still survivable throughout the year.
- Citizens living in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia Slums\|Lenturia Slums]] are often discriminated against by [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/enlightened citizens\|enlightened citizens]].
- Most citizens living in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia Slums\|Lenturia Slums]] focus on manual work because their access to education is limited. Slavery is however prohibited and there are some worker protections in place.
