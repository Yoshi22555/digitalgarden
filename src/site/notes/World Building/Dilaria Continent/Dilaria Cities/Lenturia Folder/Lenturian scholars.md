- Scholars from [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/flying universities of Lenturia\|flying universities of Lenturia]], most prefer more academical forms of studies (especially history) but there are some who venture out and explore to gather knowledge.
- Despite not being as skilled in magic as high sorcerers or members of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/enlightened court\|enlightened court]] [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturian scholars\|Lenturian scholars]] are very respected especially by very religious Lenturians because their pursuit of knowledge fits well into the ideals of personal growth.
