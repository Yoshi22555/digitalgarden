- Set of 3 universities that are placed on 3 islands flying around the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenari Palace\|Lenari Palace]].
- Any of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/enlightened citizens\|enlightened citizens]] may attempt to become a student inside one of the flying universities but they need to pass difficult magical and academical exams.
- Students that are able to finish these universities are extremely proficient in magic but most are generalists. The high quality of these universities also extends to non-magical areas.
- Students who finished studying inside these universities are the most likely candidates to become new members of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/enlightened court\|enlightened court]].
