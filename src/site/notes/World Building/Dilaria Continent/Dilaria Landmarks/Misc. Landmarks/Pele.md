- Currently artificers from [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Subur folder/Subur\|Subur]] are trying to create an [[Items & Concepts/Artificial Salur\|Artificial Salur]] after they found small deposits of [[Items & Concepts/Salur\|Salur]] inside relatively young basalt deposits on [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc.
