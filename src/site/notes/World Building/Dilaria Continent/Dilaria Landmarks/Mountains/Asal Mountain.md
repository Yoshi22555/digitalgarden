- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Asal Mountain\|Asal Mountain]] became the only place on the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria\|Dilaria]] continent where [[Items & Concepts/subterranean races\|subterranean races]] live, Orcs are especially resilient to the harsh climate of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Asal Mountain\|Asal Mountain]].
