- The northern mountain range of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria\|Dilaria]]
- Made of sedimentary rocks, specifically limestone.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Tigaraja\|Tigaraja]] mountain range is the border between [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Bagus\|Bagus]] desert and [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Plains and Forests/Veruna\|Veruna]] forest.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Tigaraja\|Tigaraja]] mountains are filled with karst caves.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Tigaraja\|Tigaraja]] mountains are largely unexplored.
