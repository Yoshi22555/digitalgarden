- North-eastern desert of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/desert circle\|desert circle]] 
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Bagus\|Bagus]] known for it's shattered ground caused by drying up of under-glacial rivers, when the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc. Landmarks/Glacier Plain\|Glacier Plain]] parted due to the creation of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc. Landmarks/Artificial Sun\|Artificial Sun]].
- Currently its least shattered region is being used as a route for the [[Items & Concepts/Kiri-Bagus Train\|Kiri-Bagus Train]] which transports goods and people between [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturia\|Lenturia]] and [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]].
- Like any other desert on [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria\|Dilaria]], most large animals on [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Bagus\|Bagus]] are insectoid or crustacean, most notably [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Myriarast\|Myriarast]]s migrate here during the reproductive season.
