- Western desert of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/desert circle\|desert circle]]
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] known for its highlands made of sandstone, has the largest variety of animals and plants among all the deserts.
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert connects [[Items & Concepts/Kiri-Bagus Train\|Kiri-Bagus Train]] with [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]].
- [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] is the breeding grounds of [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Kabuto\|Kabuto]]s, [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Falkatria\|Falkatria]]s and [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Rhinophore\|Rhinophore]]s.
- The apex predator of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert is [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Naumura\|Naumura]].
