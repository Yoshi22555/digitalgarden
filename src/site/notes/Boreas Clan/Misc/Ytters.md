- Specialized strike force that only [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] can order
- Despite not being as involved in the inner workings of the clan as [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Highest Shaman\|The Highest Shaman]] or [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Haseti\|Haseti]], [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Ytters\|Ytters]] have the most unrestricted access to [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]]. 
- Their main jobs are leading raids, infiltration of the enemies and answering to the most difficult task proposed by [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]]. 
- They are chosen by [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] from people who have greatly benefited the clan either through military or academical innovation.
- There are currently 12 [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Ytters\|Ytters]] but 4 are permanently stationed at the front, rear of the clan's caravan and 2 are stationed near [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] as personal body guards.
