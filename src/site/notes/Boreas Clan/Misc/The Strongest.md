- One of the two Leaders of the [[Boreas Clan/Boreas Clan\|Boreas Clan]]
- Currently [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] is [[Boreas Clan/Members/Barig\|Barig]]
- Chosen via a contest of strength, any member of the clan can challenge [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] but they need to accept that if the challenger loses, they are exiled from the clan.
- Leader of the warriors, he makes the most important decisions in the time of war and has the last say when it comes to organization of raids.
