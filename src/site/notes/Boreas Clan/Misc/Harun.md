[[Boreas Clan/Misc/Harun\|Harun]] - is a collective name for the most important positions beneath [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]], it consists of:
- [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Highest Shaman\|The Highest Shaman]] (Currently [[Boreas Clan/Members/Seithr\|Seithr]]) - Leader of the druids and shamans of the clan, he oversees the spiritual, botanical and academical aspects of druidcraft.
- [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Ytters\|Ytters]] - Specialized strike force that only [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] can order, despite not being as involved in the inner workings of the clan as [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Highest Shaman\|The Highest Shaman]] or [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Haseti\|Haseti]], [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Ytters\|Ytters]] have the most unrestricted access to [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]]. Their main jobs are leading raids, infiltration of the enemies and answering to the most difficult task proposed by [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]]. They are chosen by [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Oldest\|The Oldest]] and [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Strongest\|The Strongest]] from people who have greatly benefited the clan either through military or academical innovation.
- [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Haseti\|Haseti]] - Army Generals, they oversee training, raids, tactics, recruitment and anything else involving warriors or scouts.
