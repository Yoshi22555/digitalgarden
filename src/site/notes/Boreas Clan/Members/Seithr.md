- Dark Green dragonborn, wears coat covered in plants and roots. his skin is marked with many druidic symbols and he always carries a stone tablet with runes on it with him.
- Seithr is currently [[Boreas Clan/Misc/The Highest Shaman\|The Highest Shaman]], a position of power that gives him the authority to command all the other Shamans, his authority can be only questioned by Aldre and Barig.
- Currently [[Boreas Clan/Members/Seithr\|Seithr]] oversees the work done by few [[Boreas Clan/Misc/Ytters\|Ytters]] who have been tasked with retrieving the lost artefacts of [[Boreas Clan/Boreas Clan\|Boreas Clan]] ancestors.
