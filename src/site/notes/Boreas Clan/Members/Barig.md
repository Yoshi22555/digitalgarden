- Dark red dragonborn - around head and 1/2 taller than most other dragonborn, very muscular.
- Wears basic armour that covers only the most vital parts except for the head. 
- Carries massive Glaive with him.
- The Strongest member of the Clan, he oversees the warriors of the clan and has the final say when it comes to combat. 
- Because of his importance he doesn't fight much in regular battles but is still a formidable fighter when shit hits the fan and he is needed. 
- Doesn't have family and he barely cares about anything other than survival of his clan.
