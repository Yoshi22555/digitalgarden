- A rare crystal that can be found in the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Sur\|Sur]] mountains.
- Main export good of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Cairan folder/Cairan\|Cairan]] which controls the mining operations across [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Mountains/Sur\|Sur]] mountains.
- It can store both store absorb heat for years, and once the crystal has been filled with the heat, it starts to steadily release it. The speed of both absorption and the release of heat is dependent on the structure and size of the crystal.
- Because of its thermal properties its commonly used for cooling during the hot seasons and when traveling through the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/desert circle\|desert circle]]
