- Categorisation of massive land animals that roam [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria\|Dilaria]] that posses [[Player Character (Private)/Mearel/Things you've learned during your time as a nomad/Fauna Mearel/Kaiju and Leviathans/Crystal heart\|Crystal heart]]s.
- Most [[Player Character (Private)/Mearel/Things you've learned during your time as a nomad/Fauna Mearel/Kaiju and Leviathans/Kaiju\|Kaiju]] are carnivores, but there are few known herbivores.
- There have been [[Player Character (Private)/Mearel/Things you've learned during your time as a nomad/Fauna Mearel/Kaiju and Leviathans/Kaiju\|Kaiju]] living underground before [[Dilaria History/Historical Events/Melting\|Melting]] but their rapid adaptations and changes to the new environment of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria\|Dilaria]] puzzles [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Lenturia Folder/Lenturian scholars\|Lenturian scholars]] who were used to seeing slow changes caused by adaptation ensured by natural selection in other animals.
