- Named by the village during the symbolic funeral of [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Letty's Story/Letty's Parents\|Letty's Parents]]
- Currently there isn't a lot of information known about it.
- Based on few notes written by her parents, that Letty found before leaving, it is a previously unknown [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Fauna/Kaiju and Leviathans/Kaiju.\|Kaiju.]] with enormous front limbs and reptilian looking head, it was stated to be around 60 feet long, but since the report was written based on the testimony of a farmer who commissioned [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Letty's Story/Letty's Parents\|Letty's Parents]] to kill that monster, the specifics are unclear and some parts of the story could be exaggerated. 
- After the fight with [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Letty's Story/Letty's Parents\|Letty's Parents]] it disappeared and there were no bodies to be found, but based on all the blood and destruction left after the fight, it is clear that they died, and most likely were eaten by the creature.
