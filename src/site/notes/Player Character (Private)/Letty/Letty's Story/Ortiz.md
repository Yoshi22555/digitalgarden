- Small village located on the south-west border of [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert.
- Populated mostly by Oniborn.
- Most citizens of this village are hunters or hunters, but some of the most skilled ones become [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Letty's Story/Kaiju hunter\|Kaiju hunter]]s
- The citizens are traditional, which is why despite being pretty much self-sufficient they still recruit new [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Letty's Story/Kaiju hunter\|Kaiju hunter]]s and take commissions for killing [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Fauna/Kaiju and Leviathans/Kaiju.\|Kaiju.]]s from other settlements especially [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Cities/Port Emas Folder/Port Emas\|Port Emas]].
- Despite their utility, most other races fear Oniborn because of their connection to the Oni.
