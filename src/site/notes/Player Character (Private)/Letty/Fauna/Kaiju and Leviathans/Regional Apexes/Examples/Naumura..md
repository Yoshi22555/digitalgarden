- 4 Winged giant insect that resembles dragon flies.
- [[Flora and Fauna/Fauna/Naumura\|Naumura]] is the [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Fauna/Kaiju and Leviathans/Regional Apexes/Regional Apex\|Regional Apex]] of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert.
- It can store and heat up water in its water sacs located across its body, which it later uses as scolding projectiles.
- Measuring up to 50 feet long with 70 feet long wingspan, they are one of the smallest [[Player Character (Private)/Letty/Fauna/Kaiju and Leviathans/Regional Apexes/Regional Apex\|Regional Apex]]es.
- They are active predators, their diet usually consists of other flying invertebrates. They are known to attack people.
- They've been observed to fly at the speeds of up to 80 km/h.
