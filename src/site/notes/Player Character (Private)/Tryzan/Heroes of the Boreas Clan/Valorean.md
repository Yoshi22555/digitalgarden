- One of the [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Heroes of the Boreas Clan/Legendary Heroes of the Boreas Clan\|legendary characters]] from the history of the Boreas Clan
- He defeated mummy lord, rakshasa and a salamander who were threatening the clan after the unification of the Boreas Clan.
- He is said to be a powerful magic user with a lot of knowledge about different monsters of [[Pre-Tenazir Era\|Pre-Tenazir Era]] Dilaria.
- Tryzan didn't know him because this specific Valorean was alive after [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Boreas Clan Unknown History/The Unifier\|The Unifier]] died but, Tryzan should remember that Valorean was actually a [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Boreas Clan Unknown History/Boreas Houses\|House]] name given to the group of dragonborn living in the highest layers of the [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Misc. Landmarks/Glacier Plain\|Glacier Plain]]. The legendary Valorean was most likely a member of this house.
