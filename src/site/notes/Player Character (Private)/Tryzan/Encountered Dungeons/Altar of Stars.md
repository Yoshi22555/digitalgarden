- Located underground in [[World Building/Dilaria Continent/Dilaria Landmarks/Deserts/Kiri\|Kiri]] desert.
- Most likely religiously significant location for ancient [[Boreas Clan/Boreas Clan\|Boreas Clan]].
- Given the information that can be found inside its library it can be estimated that the temple was created after the unification of [[Boreas Clan/Boreas Clan\|Boreas Clan]] but before the [[Dilaria History/Historical Events/Melting\|Melting]].
- Inside there are trials that seem to test ones leadership skills.
- Once someone passes all the tests they are granted access to an artefact that looks like a crystal ball with glowing dots inside it, its purpose is unknown.
- Inside the library there are books that mention multiple [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Heroes of the Boreas Clan/Legendary Heroes of the Boreas Clan\|legendary characters]] from [[Boreas Clan/Boreas Clan\|Boreas Clan]] history, including [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Heroes of the Boreas Clan/Valorean\|Valorean]] who was a powerful magic user that defeated multiple monsters that threatened the clan.
- Inside the temple there are mentions of multiple minor gods worshiped by the clan, they were most likely introduces to the culture after [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Boreas Clan Unknown History/The Unifier\|The Unifier]] died. 
- Inside the dungeon, runes used look very similar to the ancient script used during the time of [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Boreas Clan Unknown History/The Unifier\|The Unifier]] which implies that the temple was built at most only few generations after [[Player Character (Private)/Tryzan/Boreas Clan Unknown History/The Unifier\|The Unifier]] died.
- There are runes that call this temple "Altar of Stars" however the meaning of the word "Star" is unknown.
